Greg Wilson has worked in industry and academia for 35 years, and is the author or editor of several books, including Beautiful Code, The Architecture of Open Source Applications, JavaScript for Data Science, and Teaching Tech Together. He was the co-founder and first Executive Director of Software Carpentry and received ACM SIGSOFT's Influential Educator Award in 2020.
